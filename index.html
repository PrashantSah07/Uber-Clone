Frequently asked questions from drivers

Can I drive with the Uber app in my city?

Uber is available in thousands of cities around the world. Click below to see if yours is one of them.

See all cities

What conditions must I meet to drive with the Uber app?

You must be of the minimum age to drive in your city, have a transportation authorization, have an eligible mode of transportation, and provide the required documents, including a valid driver's license.

Learn more

Is the Uber app safe?

Your safety is very important to us. Uber has an international security team that does everything in its power to prevent incidents. To find out more about in-app security features, as well as safeguards such as GPS tracking and phone number anonymization, follow the link below.

Discover Uber's commitment to safety

How do I find a vehicle?

If you'd like to drive with the Uber app but don't own a car, you can get one from our partners, or from a car fleet manager in some regions. Please note that vehicle options may vary by city.

Learn more

What insurance do I need for my business?

As a VTC driver, you need to be insured to transport people for hire or reward. As such, both Operating Liability and Traffic Liability insurance are mandatory.
